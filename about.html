About Kibiko Primary School

A center of academic excellence, discipline, and innovation in Kibiko, Ngong.

School Overview

Kibiko Primary School is a public primary school located in Kibiko, Ngong, Kajiado County, Kenya. The school is committed to providing quality basic education that nurtures academic excellence, good character, and lifelong learning.

Through a dedicated teaching staff and a supportive learning environment, the school prepares learners to become responsible, skilled, and confident citizens.

Our Mission

To provide quality, inclusive, and holistic education that empowers learners academically, morally, and socially while embracing innovation and technology.

Our Vision

To be a leading primary school recognized for academic excellence, discipline, and the development of innovative, responsible learners.

Our Motto & Core Values

Motto:
“Do everything good to your very best.”

  • Excellence in learning
  • Integrity and discipline
  • Respect and responsibility
  • Creativity and innovation
  • Teamwork and community service

ICT & Innovation Focus

Kibiko Primary School integrates Information and Communication Technology (ICT) into learning to enhance digital literacy, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

Through the ICT Club, learners gain exposure to computer skills, coding basics, and innovative projects that prepare them for the digital world.
